About

A vampire themed game where the player plays as a vampire attempting to make it big with a pizza tourist trap town. The player will establish, name, design, upgrade, and progress their town with money earned from making and selling pizza. Because vampires are immortal, the player is able to play through various eras of time and experience technological advancements. All while remaining low profile as a vampire, the player's goal is to become more powerful through pizza hungry tourist blood and money.

Gameplay Genre: 2D Isometric Tycoon Resource-Based Farming Simulator
Game Engine: Unity (C#)
