About

Purpose/Introduction: You play as a freshly turned vampire attempting to establish a town with a focus on pizza making. All while remaining low profile as a vampire, you will become more powerful through pizza hungry tourist blood and money. As an immortal pizza pioneer, you will progress your town with revolutionary technology through time.

Gameplay Genre: 2D Isometric Tycoon Resource-Based Farming Simulator
Storyboard Theme/Genre: Silly, Gothic
Game Engine: Unity (C#)
